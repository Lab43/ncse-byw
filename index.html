Billion-Year Walk

February 16, 2014
9am to 3pm
Rotary Nature Center
600 Bellevue Avenue
Oakland, CA 94612
Get Directions

Take the whole family back a billion years and walk forward to the present around Lake Merritt.

Follow dozens of signs describing the history of our Earth and its life. Docents from UC Berkeley and NCSE will answer your questions as you travel through time around the lake and back to the Rotary Nature Center.

The total distance for the walk is 3.1 miles / 5 kilometers.

Snacks, drinks, and goodies for the kids while they last. Test your knowledge and take a trivia quiz.

Millions of dinosaurs and other animals were harmed in the making of this history!
